FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need a credit card to start?▾
No. Sign up and use InvoiceFlow free with up to 3 invoices. No credit card required. The 14-day Pro trial also starts without a card.
Can I cancel anytime?▾
Yes. Cancel anytime from billing settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
Does my client need an account to view their invoice?▾
No. You share a secret link and your client can view and download the invoice without creating an account or logging in.
Which currencies are supported?▾
InvoiceFlow supports USD, EUR, IDR (Indonesian Rupiah), GBP, SGD, AUD, JPY, and MYR.
Can I use InvoiceFlow in Indonesia?▾
Absolutely. InvoiceFlow was built with Indonesian freelancers in mind — IDR currency, WhatsApp sending, and local bank transfer as payment method.
Is my data safe?▾
Yes. Your data is stored securely and protected with Row Level Security — only you can access your invoices and client data. We never share or sell your data.
